About us


Welcome to Namalundu Secondary School, a beacon of educational excellence nestled in the heart of Chinkata Province.
Founded on the principles of fostering holistic development and nurturing the leaders of tomorrow, Namalundu Secondary
stands as a testament to the transformative power of education. At Namalundu, we believe that every student possesses unique talents
and potential waiting to be unleashed. With a dedicated team of experienced educators, state-of-the-art facilities,
and a supportive community, we strive to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally.



As we embark on this journey of learning and discovery together, we invite you to join us
in shaping the future and making a difference in the world.
Whether you are a student, parent, educator, or community member, we welcome you
to the Namalundu family, where every dream is possible, and every success is celebrated.
